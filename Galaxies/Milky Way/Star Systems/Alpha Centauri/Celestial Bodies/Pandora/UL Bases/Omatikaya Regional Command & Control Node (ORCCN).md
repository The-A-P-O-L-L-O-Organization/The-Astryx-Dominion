## **Site Selection & Philosophy**
- **Location:** Floating ridge just outside visual and spiritual radius of the Tree of Voices; elevated position avoids shadowing, avoids interfering with Eywa-linked ritual pathways, and maintains unobtrusive presence.
- **Design Ethos:** “Biomimetic Overlay” — base integrates with cliff faces and airborne flora. Structures are suspended using tensioned pylons anchored to bedrock, reducing footprint and avoiding root network disruption.
- **Cultural Considerations:** Liaison with Omatikaya elders and GC-approved anthropologists determined safe zones; ceremonial pathways preserved; no direct structures under or immediately adjacent to sacred trees.
