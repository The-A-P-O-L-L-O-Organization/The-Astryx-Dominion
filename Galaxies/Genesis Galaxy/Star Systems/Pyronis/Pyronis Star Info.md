These flares, though hazardous, provide **massive bursts of electromagnetic energy** — ideal for **UL energy harvesting protocols** and **machine evolution experiments** under the HEPHAESTUS Subfunction.

### **2. Stellar Anomalies & Phenomena**

**A. Magneto-Stellar Convergence Zones**  
Localized zones of amplified electromagnetic interference form around Pyronis every 42 planetary cycles. Within these regions, communication blackouts occur — but energy harvesting spikes by nearly 300%. These “convergence storms” are monitored by HEPHAESTUS satellites and AI subroutines for pattern prediction.

**B. Stellar Resonance Layer (SRL)**  
Pyronis’s chromosphere demonstrates rhythmic plasma oscillations that resonate with certain metallic frequencies. UL scientists theorize that **machine alloys produced within Pyronis-Vulcan’s Cauldron** are subtly influenced by these resonances — granting **enhanced magnetic stability** and **electrical adaptability**.

**C. Core Flux Waves**  
Deep solar seismic activity occasionally generates “core flux waves” — gravity distortions that briefly alter the orbits of inner-system satellites. These waves are tracked by MINERVA for gravitational data collection and used as **stress-test events** for AI-piloted stabilizers in the Cauldron Spire.
