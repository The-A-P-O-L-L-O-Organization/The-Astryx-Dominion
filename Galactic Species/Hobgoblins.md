**Species File — Hobgoblins**

**Homeworld:** Tharkun  
**Galaxy:** Andromeda  
**Dominant Star-Nation(s):**

- **United Defense** — Hobgoblins excel in tactical warfare, battlefield coordination, and martial training programs.
    
- **United Robotics** — They contribute to automated defense systems, drone tactics, and mechanized combat platforms.
    
- **United Transit** — Skilled in logistics, supply chain management, and rapid deployment for strategic operations.
    

---

### **Overview**

Hobgoblins are taller and more muscular than standard goblins, with disciplined physiques, strong endurance, and sharp tactical minds. They possess a natural aptitude for military strategy, organized teamwork, and industrial labor.

**Physiological Traits:**

- Height: 1.6–2.0 meters
    
- Musculature: Dense and durable, optimized for strength and endurance
    
- Dexterity: Moderate, with high coordination in coordinated movements and weapon handling
    
- Senses: Acute visual and auditory perception for situational awareness and tactical evaluation
    

---

### **Homeworld: Tharkun**

- **Type:** Rocky, semi-arid plains interspersed with mountain ranges and fortified settlements.
    
- **Gravity:** 1.05g — slightly heavier than Earth, enhancing natural muscular development.
    
- **Atmosphere:** Earth-like; breathable with minimal adjustments.
    
- **Average Surface Temp:** 18–32°C
    

**Ecosystems:**

- Expansive rocky plains suitable for training maneuvers and mechanized vehicle trials.
    
- Sparse forests and canyons providing natural defensive positions and ambush opportunities.
    
- Industrialized regions for manufacturing, armament testing, and defensive infrastructure.
    

**Native Fauna:**

- Razorback Raptors — aggressive, pack-oriented predators adapted to Tharkun’s terrain.
    
- Cliffhorn Beasts — sure-footed grazers used in military training exercises.
    
- Ironhide Scarabs — armored insects occasionally used in bioengineering or hazard testing.
    

**Environmental Traits:**

- Hobgoblins thrive in harsh terrain, industrialized regions, and organized military compounds.
    
- Natural endurance and strength make them suitable for both manual labor and battlefield applications.
    

---

### **Cultural Characteristics**

- **Core Ethos:** “Strength, discipline, unity.”
    
- Societies are militaristic and hierarchical, with emphasis on strategy, endurance, and training.
    
- Architecture emphasizes defensive strength: fortified citadels, training grounds, and secure logistical hubs.
    
- Rituals often involve martial training, strategic competitions, and merit-based progression.
    

---

### **Star-Nation Integration**

**United Defense:**

- Lead tactical training, battlefield simulations, and military strategy development.
    
- Hobgoblins excel in commanding units, defensive planning, and crisis coordination.
    

**United Robotics:**

- Design and maintain automated defense drones, exosuits, and combat-enhancement systems.
    
- Work closely with United Defense for integrated mechanized operations.
    

**United Transit:**

- Specialize in tactical logistics, rapid deployment systems, and supply chain optimization for military campaigns.
    

---

### **Technological & Tactical Specialization**

- **Combat Strategy:** Skilled planners for coordinated engagements, defensive fortifications, and unit management.
    
- **Mechanized Operations:** Integrate automated drones, vehicles, and exosuits into operational strategy.
    
- **Endurance & Strength:** Naturally suited for long missions, heavy lifting, and high-intensity physical tasks.
    
- **Neural Implants:** Provide live translation, tactical overlays, and integration with UL command networks.
    

---

### **Mutagenic Adaptation Notes**

**Project: Tharkun Vanguard:**

- **Safe Dose:** Enhances physical strength, reflexes, endurance, tactical reasoning, and situational awareness.
    
- **Overdose:** Produces superhuman strength, extreme reflexes, temporary enhanced cognition for battlefield multitasking, and accelerated recovery; neural implants required for coordination and control.
    

---

### **Modern Integration**

- **Military Operations:** Lead tactical simulations, field operations, and strategic defense projects.
    
- **Robotics & Automation:** Collaborate with mechanized systems for optimized battlefield performance.
    
- **Logistics & Deployment:** Oversee complex supply chains, troop movements, and rapid-response units.
    

---

### **Cultural Interactions**

- Hobgoblins frequently coordinate with goblins, humans, and orcs for joint military and defense initiatives.
    
- Their disciplined, hierarchical culture can clash with improvisational or chaotic species, requiring oversight for cooperative projects.
    
- Traditions of strategic games, combat trials, and merit-based hierarchy inform their societal interactions.
    

---

### **Notes for Campaign Integration**

- Hobgoblins can serve as military instructors, security specialists, or strategic planners for players to interact with.
    
- Tharkun offers rugged terrain, fortified installations, and organized settlements for adventure, training, or combat scenarios.
    
- Project: Tharkun Vanguard mutagen overdoses can create plot hooks involving enhanced warriors, temporary battlefield geniuses, or rogue super-soldiers.
