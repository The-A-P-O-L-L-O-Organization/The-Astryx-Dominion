**Species File — Shifters**

**Homeworld:** _Lyriath_  
**Galaxy:** Milky Way  
**Dominant Star-Nation(s):**

- **United Cognition** — Shifters excel in adaptive intelligence, field analysis, and environmental sensing.
    
- **United Defense** — Their agility and partial animal traits make them ideal for reconnaissance, infiltration, and rapid-response squads.
    
- **United BioWorks** — Shifters are often involved in hybrid physiology research, adaptive mutagen trials, and biomonitoring systems.
    

---

### **Overview**

Shifters are humanoid beings descended from lycanthropic lineages, capable of exhibiting minor animalistic traits such as enhanced senses, claws, or temporary fur growth. Their physical adaptability varies individually, often reflecting their genetic heritage. Shifters are naturally agile, perceptive, and resilient, combining human-like intellect with animal instincts.

**Physiological Traits:**

- Height: 1.6–2.0 meters
    
- Weight: 60–100 kg
    
- Animalistic Features: Can include claws, partial tails, fangs, heightened senses, or fur patches
    
- Reflexes: Exceptional agility and coordination, capable of rapid acrobatic maneuvers
    
- Senses: Superior hearing, smell, and night vision relative to baseline humans
    

---

### **Homeworld: Lyriath**

- **Type:** Temperate planet with dense forests, rolling plains, and riverine ecosystems
    
- **Gravity:** 0.98–1.0g
    
- **Atmosphere:** Oxygen-nitrogen mix; highly breathable
    
- **Average Surface Temp:** 14–28°C
    

**Ecosystems:**

- Dense forests teeming with medium and large fauna
    
- Wetlands and river systems supporting amphibious and aquatic species
    
- Rolling plains suited for grazing species and hunting grounds
    

**Native Fauna:**

- _Moonfang Wolves_ — pack predators whose behavior influenced early Shifter hunting instincts
    
- _Glider Raptorbirds_ — small winged predators, enhancing arboreal adaptability
    
- _Furback Boars_ — sturdy, resilient herbivores that roam plains and forests
    

**Environmental Traits:**

- Shifters are naturally attuned to forests, plains, and mixed ecosystems
    
- Their partial animal adaptations allow them to traverse rough terrain efficiently, track targets, and detect threats with heightened senses
    

---

### **Cultural Characteristics**

- **Core Ethos:** _“Adapt, survive, evolve.”_
    
- Societies are tribal and clan-based, emphasizing cooperation, environmental attunement, and skill mastery
    
- Ritual transformations and coming-of-age ceremonies often involve demonstrating animalistic prowess or partial shifting
    
- Architecture favors flexible dwellings integrated with natural landscapes: treetop platforms, hidden glades, and camouflaged villages
    

---

### **Star-Nation Integration**

**United Cognition:**

- Shifters serve as scouts, environmental monitors, and adaptive intelligence analysts
    
- Their instincts and animal traits allow them to detect anomalies, hazards, and potential threats that others may miss
    

**United Defense:**

- Reconnaissance units, infiltration teams, and tactical rapid-response forces rely on their speed, agility, and stealth
    
- Partial shifting allows operations in environments hostile to fully human personnel
    

**United BioWorks:**

- Involved in biomonitoring, mutagen trials, and hybrid enhancement programs
    
- Capable of testing and piloting experimental bio-adaptations safely
    

---

### **Technological & Tactical Specialization**

- **Stealth & Recon:** Utilize partial animal traits and natural camouflage to infiltrate or surveil
    
- **Rapid Response:** Exceptional agility and reflexes make them effective in fast-paced operations
    
- **Adaptive Physiology:** Minor animal traits assist in climbing, swimming, or enhanced endurance
    
- **Neural Implants:** Provide live translation, override integration for mutagenic enhancements, and real-time environmental data coordination
    
- **Preferred Tactics:** Ambush, tracking, environmental manipulation, and rapid hit-and-run maneuvers
    

---

### **Mutagenic Adaptation Notes**

**Project: Lyriath Predator** — UL’s mutagenic enhancement program for Shifters:

- **Safe Dose:** Enhances reflexes, strength, senses, and limited shifting ability
    
- **Overdose:** Triggers extreme morphological changes, including full partial transformations, temporary size augmentation, or heightened sensory overload; neural implants required to prevent uncontrollable behavior
    
- **Control Integration:** Implants coordinate shifting, task execution, and environmental awareness
    

---

### **Modern Integration**

- **Recon & Tactical Ops:** Shifters excel in reconnaissance, search-and-rescue, and high-risk infiltration
    
- **Environmental Research:** Conduct biomonitoring, hybrid physiology experiments, and adaptive ecosystem studies
    
- **Mutagenic Testbeds:** Used to trial experimental adaptive mutagens and observe controlled overdoses under UL supervision
    

---

### **Cultural Interactions**

- Shifters often collaborate with **Goblins**, **Aarakocra**, and **Humans** for reconnaissance and exploratory projects
    
- Tribal leadership structures are integrated into UL’s field operations, respecting Shifter hierarchy while ensuring operational coordination
    
- Communities maintain ritualized partial shifting as a cultural and training exercise
    

---

### **Notes for Campaign Integration**

- Shifters can serve as scouts, infiltrators, or hybrid experiment NPCs
    
- Lyriath provides an environment for wilderness survival, espionage, or biomonitoring missions
    
- Mutagen overdose scenarios may lead to unpredictable transformations, enhanced capabilities, or behavioral anomalies
