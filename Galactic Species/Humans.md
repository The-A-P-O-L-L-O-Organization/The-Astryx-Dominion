**Species File — Humans**

**Homeworld:** Terra  
**Galaxy:** Milky Way  
**Dominant Star-Nation(s):**

- **United Laboratories** — Humans provide the core of research, innovation, and hybridization projects across multiple disciplines.
    
- **United Engineering** — Skilled in industrial design, structural engineering, and cross-species technology integration.
    
- **United Transit** — Lead developers of interplanetary and interstellar transport systems.
    
- **United Defense** — Contribute military strategies, tactical leadership, and command-level coordination.
    

---

### **Overview**

Humans are bipedal, highly adaptable, and culturally versatile. They possess moderate physical attributes compared to other species but compensate with ingenuity, problem-solving, and complex social structures. Their ability to adapt to diverse environments and collaborate with other species makes them a dominant force across star systems.

**Physiological Traits:**

- Height: 1.5–2.0 meters
    
- Musculature: Moderate, capable of developing strength and endurance with training
    
- Dexterity: Good fine motor skills, adaptable to a variety of tasks
    
- Senses: Standard vision, hearing, and smell; enhanced with prosthetics or neural implants
    

---

### **Homeworld: Terra (Earth)**

- **Type:** Rocky, water-rich planet with diverse ecosystems
    
- **Gravity:** 1g
    
- **Atmosphere:** Nitrogen-oxygen mixture; breathable without modifications
    
- **Average Surface Temp:** 14–30°C depending on region
    

**Ecosystems:**

- Forests, grasslands, deserts, mountains, and urbanized areas
    
- Oceans, lakes, and river systems supporting abundant aquatic life
    
- Human-built settlements ranging from megacities to rural habitats
    

**Native Fauna & Flora:**

- Diverse animal species including mammals, birds, reptiles, and insects
    
- Wide range of plant life from forests to agricultural crops
    
- Adapted and genetically enhanced species through early bioengineering efforts
    

**Environmental Traits:**

- Humans are highly adaptable to different climates and urban or natural environments
    
- Skilled in resource management, construction, and technology use
    

---

### **Cultural Characteristics**

- **Core Ethos:** “Adapt, innovate, collaborate.”
    
- Societies are highly variable: democratic, authoritarian, tribal, and corporate structures exist
    
- Emphasis on education, technological innovation, exploration, and diplomacy
    
- Architecture ranges from classical to ultra-modern, with integrated technological systems
    

---

### **Star-Nation Integration**

**United Laboratories:**

- Lead research in hybridization, AI, robotics, and quantum computing
    
- Develop protocols for cross-species collaboration and technological adaptation
    

**United Engineering:**

- Human engineers excel at infrastructure, interstellar construction, and machinery design
    
- Manage large-scale industrial and scientific projects across colonies
    

**United Transit:**

- Responsible for designing interplanetary transport, warp-capable vessels, and supply chains
    
- Optimize civilian and military mobility across star systems
    

**United Defense:**

- Humans contribute tactical planning, fleet command, and strategic coordination
    
- Serve in leadership roles for multi-species defense operations
    

---

### **Technological & Tactical Specialization**

- Engineering, scientific research, and cross-disciplinary problem-solving
    
- Diplomacy and leadership across multi-species alliances
    
- Adaptable tactics for both conventional and hybridized combat scenarios
    
- Neural implants: provide live translation, environmental scanning, and coordination with UL systems
    

---

### **Mutagenic Adaptation Notes**

**Project: Terra Initiative:**

- **Safe Dose:** Enhances reflexes, cognition, stamina, and adaptive capabilities for space or hybrid missions
    
- **Overdose:** Produces extreme physical augmentation, accelerated intelligence, and temporary advanced perception; implants required for safe control and integration
    

---

### **Modern Integration**

- Humans serve as the backbone of research, colonization, and interstellar governance
    
- Adapt quickly to alien worlds, collaborate with multiple species, and manage hybrid technologies
    
- Take lead roles in strategic planning, scientific innovation, and exploratory operations
    

---

### **Cultural Interactions**

- Humans often act as diplomats, coordinators, and integrators among other species
    
- Their adaptability and problem-solving make them natural allies for species with specialized skills
    
- Societal conflicts may arise due to cultural diversity, but humans are generally capable negotiators
    

---

### **Notes for Campaign Integration**

- Humans provide familiar grounding for players, both culturally and physiologically
    
- Terra offers a wide variety of environments, from cities to wilderness, for adventure, research, or diplomacy
    
- Project: Terra Initiative mutagen overdoses can create plot hooks for superhuman explorers, rogue enhanced humans, or accelerated technological breakthroughs
