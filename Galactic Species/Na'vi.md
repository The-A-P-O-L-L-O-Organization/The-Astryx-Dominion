Na’vi

Medium humanoid (Na’vi), typically neutral good

Ability Score Increase

Your Dexterity increases by 2 and your Wisdom increases by 1.

Age

Na’vi mature at a similar rate to humans but tend to live longer—up to 150 years.

Alignment

Generally neutral good, deeply connected to their clans, culture, and the planet’s spirit.

Size

Medium. Most adult Na’vi stand around 9 feet tall with slender, athletic builds. 

Speed

Base walking speed is 35 ft.

Darkvision

Thanks to their nocturnal-adapted eyes, you can see in dim light within 60 feet as if it were bright light, and in darkness as if it were dim light. You cannot discern color in darkness, only shades of gray. 

Keen Senses

You have proficiency in the Perception skill.

Prehensile Tail

Your long, prehensile tail grants you exceptional balance and coordination:

Advantage on Dexterity (Acrobatics) checks when balancing or climbing.

You can use your tail to manipulate objects (such as opening unlocked doors, retrieving items, etc.).


Bioluminescent Expression

Unique bioluminescent patterns on your skin glow in low light, shifting intensity with emotional states. This functions as a mood indicator in social interaction, granting advantage on Charisma (Insight) checks made to read your emotions.

Natural Connection (Tsaheylu)

Once per long rest, you can bind your neural braid to an animal's (or beast’s) braid or neural interface, granting you limited empathic communication and allowing you to coordinate actions or share senses (as DM allows).

Carbon-Fiber Skeleton

Your bones are reinforced with naturally occurring carbon-fiber-like structure—granting resistance to nonmagical bludgeoning damage. 

Language & Culture

You can speak, read, and write Common and one additional language. Their language incorporates infixes and is rooted in natural metaphors. 

Lifespan & Strength
Life expectancy around 120–150 years. Despite their lean figures, Na’vi possess roughly quadruple the strength of an average human.
