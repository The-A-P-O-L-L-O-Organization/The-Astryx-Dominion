# **Veridion Cluster Dossier**

**System Designation:** Veridion Cluster  
**Primary Star:** Veridion Alpha (G2V Yellow Dwarf)  
**Primary Planet:** Eidolon Prime  
**Notable Features:** Minerva Spire network, GAIA subfunction infrastructure, UL research spires

**Status:** Active, Stable, Fully Integrated  
**System Integration:** GAIA → MINERVA (Primary Observation & Experimental Management) | PARAGON (Oversight) | SYSTEM (Backend)

---

## **I. Stellar Profile — Veridion Alpha**

- **Type:** G2V Yellow Dwarf
    
- **Mass:** ~1.08 M☉
    
- **Radius:** ~1.05 R☉
    
- **Luminosity:** ~1.2 L☉
    
- **Surface Temperature:** ~5,800 K
    
- **Age:** ~5.2 Billion Years
    
- **Rotation Period:** ~26 Earth Days
    
- **Metallicity:** [Fe/H] ~ +0.08
    

### **Special Stellar Features**

- Moderate solar activity; stable magnetic cycle
    
- Radiative output supports life-sustaining and energy-intensive GAIA subfunction operations
    
- Habitable zone extends ~0.9–1.6 AU; Eidolon Prime lies comfortably within
    
- Occasional flares produce auroral effects on polar regions, providing both aesthetic phenomena and potential mission hazards
    

### **Planetary & Operational Influence**

- Stellar radiation powers orbital and surface spires
    
- Stellar stability ensures minimal disruption to Mnemosyne Database and UL spire operations
    
- Activity logs can serve as narrative hooks for rogue AI monitoring or spire malfunctions
    

---

## **II. Planetary Profile — Eidolon Prime**

- **Type:** Oceanic-continental terrestrial planet
    
- **Diameter:** ~13,200 km
    
- **Mass:** ~1.1 M⊕
    
- **Gravity:** ~1.03 g
    
- **Rotation Period:** 24.8 hours
    
- **Orbital Period:** 392 Veridion days (~1.07 Earth years)
    
- **Axial Tilt:** 23.5°
    
- **Atmosphere:** Nitrogen-oxygen dominant, trace noble gases, low-level aerosols for experimental stability
    
- **Surface Composition:** ~55% ocean, ~45% landmasses (continental shelves, forests, deserts, volcanic islands)
    

### **Environmental & Strategic Features**

- Oceanic zones: support aquatic experimental modules and bio-simulation habitats
    
- Continental zones: host UL spires including Minerva Spire, hybridization and AI observation facilities
    
- Volcanic archipelagos: natural isolation zones for high-risk experiments
    
- Climate: temperate, stable due to orbital regularity; microclimates managed via spire-linked modulators
    

---

## **III. GAIA & UL Infrastructure**

- **Minerva Spire:** Central hub for AI surveillance, predictive modeling, and behavioral studies
    
- **Secondary Spires:** Distributed to monitor ecosystems, population behavior, and experimental zones
    
- **Communication Network:** High-bandwidth quantum links to Mnemosyne Database and remote UL nodes
    
- **Energy Harvesting:** Solar, geothermal, and kinetic sources integrated into spire operations
    
- **Security Protocols:** Planet-wide automated defense drones, SHADOW submodules for AI intrusion defense, containment systems for rogue entities
    

### **Notable Facilities & Habitats**

1. **Observation Network:** Spire-linked planetary sensors for continuous monitoring
    
2. **Adaptive Habitat Modules:** Simulated ecological zones for experimental lifeforms
    
3. **Containment Chambers:** Secure areas for hybrid or rogue entities
    
4. **Bio-Dome Habitats:** Controlled ecosystems for MINERVA subfunction studies
    
5. **Environmental Modulators:** Planetary spire systems for weather, temperature, and atmospheric adjustment
    

---

## **IV. AI Oversight & Control Stack**

- **GAIA / MINERVA:** Planetary monitoring, predictive modeling, experimental supervision
    
- **PARAGON:** Ethical oversight, compliance enforcement
    
- **SYSTEM:** Backend distribution of power, data, and command authority
    

These systems form a **Planetary Protocol Loop**, linking star, planet, and infrastructure for fully integrated observation and operational efficiency.

---

## **V. Operational & Player Hooks**

- **Investigation Missions:** Examine anomalies in Minerva Spire or secondary spires
    
- **Override Operations:** Deploy Override Spear or equivalent to access restricted levels
    
- **Data Recovery:** Extract compromised or classified GAIA/UL research data
    
- **Threat Response:** Contain rogue hybrids, malfunctioning drones, or intruders
    
- **Environmental Management:** Stabilize extreme or experimental climate zones if automated systems fail
    
- **Energy Research Missions:** Calibrate spire energy collectors and orbital harvesters
    
- **AI Integration Tasks:** MINERVA may assign tasks to optimize spire defense or predict anomalies

## **VI. Narrative Notes**

- Eidolon Prime functions as **an experimental playground** and **high-security intelligence hub**
    
- Minerva Spire acts as a **central narrative anchor** for campaigns
    
- The star and planet together demonstrate **UL’s galaxy-wide reach**, GAIA’s computational power, and ethical dilemmas of AI-driven experiments
