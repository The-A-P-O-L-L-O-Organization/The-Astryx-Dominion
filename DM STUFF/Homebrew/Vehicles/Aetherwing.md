### **Aetherwing — Personal Multi-Environment Vehicle**

_Vehicle (hover-bike), requires 1 rider_

---

**Armor Class:** 16 (reinforced UL alloy frame)  
**Hit Points:** 50 (structural integrity; critical at 0 HP)  
**Speed:**

- Hover: 120 ft.
    
- Flying: 60 ft. (hover capable)
    
- Swimming: 60 ft.
    
- Max altitude: 20,000 ft. (without upgrade)
    

**Capacity:** 1 rider

---

### **Environment Envelope**

- Rider is protected by a **Bio-Respiratory Field**:
    
    - Can breathe underwater or in low-oxygen environments.
        
    - Provides advantage on Constitution saving throws against environmental hazards (poisonous gases, thin air).
        
- **Vacuum Adaptation Upgrade:** allows operation in open space, radiation protection, and life support for up to 48 hours.
    

---

### **Controls**

- **Neural-link handlebars**: Dex (Vehicle) check to control.
    
- **Optional Queue-Link Socket:** if a hybrid with a queue is riding, they can use Intelligence (Arcana) or Wisdom (Animal Handling) to synchronize maneuvers.
    
- **Manual Override:** DC 12 Dex check to regain control after turbulence, collision, or system malfunction.
    

---

### **Attacks**

- **Integrated Pulse Blaster (optional weapon module):**
    
    - Range: 60 ft.
        
    - Damage: 2d8 lightning or force damage (switchable).
        
    - Attack: +5 to hit (Dex-based).
        
    - Recharge: 1 round between shots.
        

---

### **Special Traits**

- **Hover Stability:** Can hover in place without movement. Advantage on checks to resist being moved by wind or water currents.
    
- **Rapid Maneuver:** Once per short rest, can Dash as a bonus action without provoking opportunity attacks.
    
- **Submersible:** No penalties to movement underwater. Can survive at depths up to 1,000 ft.
    
- **Environmental Adaptation:** Automatically adjusts temperature, pressure, and oxygen for rider comfort.
    

---

### **Vehicle Damage & Effects**

- **Critical Damage:** At 0 HP, the Aetherwing loses all movement and collapses its envelope. Rider must succeed on DC 15 Dex save or take 3d10 bludgeoning damage from crash.
    
- **Minor Damage (25–50% HP):** Max speed reduced by half; advantage on Dex checks to resist envelope stress.
    
- **Severe Damage (<25% HP):** Systems unstable — Dex saving throws (DC 15) required at the start of each turn to maintain control.
    

---

### **Optional Features (DM-controlled upgrades)**

- **Vacuum Package:** full life support and limited orbital maneuvering.
    
- **Weapon Module Expansion:** can add missile pods, non-lethal stunners, or additional pulse blasters.
    
- **Queue Synchronization Upgrade:** allows hybrid riders to extend their senses through the vehicle, giving advantage on Perception and Initiative checks while riding.
