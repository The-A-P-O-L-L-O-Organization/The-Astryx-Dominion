**UL Currency System**:

- Credit Micro (Cm): Smallest Unit, equivalent to a Copper Piece (CP).
- Credit Unit (Cu): Standard minor unit, equivalent to a Silver Piece (SP).
- Credit Fraction (Cf): Mid-tier, equivalent to an Electrum Piece (EP).
- Credit Standard (Cr): Common, high-value currency, equivalent to a Gold Piece (GP).
- Credit Core (CC): Rare, ultra-valuable, equivalent to a Platinum Piece (PP).

Conversion Example:
- 100 Cn = 10 Cm = 1 Cu
- 2 Cu = 1 Cf
- 5 Cf = 1 Cr
- 10 Cr = 1 CC
