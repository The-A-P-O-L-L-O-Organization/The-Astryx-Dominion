---

### 1. Overview

The **Andromedan Council (AC)** is a **multi-nation federation** located in the Andromeda Galaxy, composed of several sovereign star-nations operating under a shared governance framework.

The AC is a **member of the Galactic Community (GC)**, recognizing its laws and mandates but maintaining internal autonomy in governance, military operations, and interstellar policy.

UL classifies the AC as a **federated mid-tier power** with strong internal cohesion and regional influence, particularly within Andromeda.

---

### 2. Core Mandate

The Andromedan Council’s purpose is to:

- Coordinate defense and diplomatic initiatives between member star-nations
    
- Standardize interstellar trade, technology sharing, and resource management within Andromeda
    
- Facilitate arbitration and conflict resolution among members
    
- Serve as a GC representative, voting collectively on matters affecting the Andromeda Galaxy
    

The Council maintains **internal legislative and judicial structures**, ensuring compliance among its constituent star-nations.

---

### 3. Institutional Structure

#### 3.1 Council Assembly

- Composed of representatives from each member star-nation
    
- Makes policy decisions via **majority or supermajority voting**
    
- Oversees interstellar treaties, trade agreements, and joint military operations
    

#### 3.2 Judicial Committee

- Handles internal disputes between member star-nations
    
- Coordinates with the GC Judicial Tribunal when conflicts intersect with multi-galactic law
    

#### 3.3 Defense Directorate

- Maintains joint military coordination and fleet oversight
    
- Ensures rapid collective response to external threats
    
- Defense capabilities focus on **Andromeda Galaxy security**, not unilateral expansion
    

#### 3.4 Diplomatic Secretariat

- Liaison with GC and other intergalactic factions
    
- Manages cross-galaxy agreements, trade deals, and information sharing
    

---

### 4. GC Membership & Compliance

- **Member Status:** Full GC participant
    
- **Compliance Nature:** AC generally enforces GC laws internally but operates with **de facto autonomy** in regional matters
    
- **Delegated Enforcement:** Enforcement of multi-galactic GC rulings within Andromeda may rely on the AC’s own fleets or coordination with capable GC members, including UL if necessary
    
- **Strategic Role:** Acts as a regional stabilizer, aligning Andromeda interests with GC-wide mandates
    

---

### 5. United Laboratories — Relationship

- UL interacts with AC as **both GC peer and independent actor**
    
- UL participates in GC negotiations with AC representation, often influencing multi-galactic policy outcomes
    
- AC recognizes UL’s **paragovernmental authority** and operational reach, which can shape AC compliance behavior
    
- Potential friction exists if UL projects in Andromeda intersect with AC jurisdiction, though conflicts are mitigated through diplomatic channels
    

---

### 6. Political & Strategic Assessment

- **Power Vector:** Collective diplomacy, regional defense, GC participation
    
- **Operational Strength:** Moderate — strong fleets in Andromeda, coordinated trade and technology policy
    
- **Direct Threat to UL:** Low, unless political objectives conflict
    
- **Utility to UL:** High — facilitates intergalactic diplomacy, information sharing, and GC-backed interventions
    
- **Vulnerability:** Regional cohesion is dependent on member cooperation; dissenting star-nations can slow decision-making
