### **Purpose:**

The UL Board is the **central governing council** for all corporate, operational, and strategic decisions. While subsidiaries operate with high autonomy, the Board ensures alignment with **UL’s long-term vision**, intergalactic policy, and research ethics (where applicable).

---

### **Composition:**

1. **Founder**
    - Observational role only.
    - Cannot vote, but holds **veto authority** over any board decision.
    - Guides the board philosophically and strategically, sets vision, approves ultra-classified initiatives.
    - Info about him can be found at [[Founder Public Info]] and [[Founder Info (WIP)]]
    
2. **Subsidiary Representatives**
    - One rep per UL subsidiary:
        - **United Tech, United Robotics, United BioWorks, United Quantum, United Security, United Transit, United Terraformation, United Education, United Comms, United Games, United Engineering, United Cognition, United Archives, etc...**
    - Representatives must have **Omega clearance**.
    - They vote on matters directly affecting their subsidiary, research approvals, and cross-subsidiary projects.
    - Present operational and fiscal reports quarterly.
3. **Departmental Representatives**
    - Core UL departments are represented on the board to ensure internal compliance and governance:
        - **Ethics Department:** Reviews and advises on ethical implications of research and operations (e.g., Morrow, hybrids).
        - **HR Department:** Advises on employee welfare, labor deployment, and internal security policies.
        - **Financial Department:** Oversees budgeting, resource allocation, and audits.
        - **Protocol Department:** Monitors, reviews, and recommends activation or creation of protocols.
        - **Legal & Contracts Department:** Ensures all subsidiary and employee contracts are maintained and enforced.
        - **Security & Override Department:** Advises on Override Spear usage, hybrid containment, and classified emergency measures.
        - **Data & Archives Department:** Maintains Genesis Vault, Singularity, and other critical data repositories.
    
4. **Special Advisory Panels** (Non-voting)
    - **Aeonborn Envoys:** Provide insight on cross-species ethics, alien cooperation, and sentient rights in UL operations.
    - **System / GAIA Liaison:** Advises the board on AI and environmental intelligence integrations.
    - **Protocol Oversight AI:** Tracks all protocol activations in real time for board awareness.
    

---

### **Voting Structure:**
- Subsidiary and departmental representatives each have **one vote**.
- Founder observes and can **veto any motion**, even if the majority votes in favor.
- Certain decisions (e.g., activation of galaxy-scale protocols, Singularity adjustments, or Morrow triggers) require **supermajority + Founder approval**.


---

### **Meetings:**
- Frequency: **Quarterly for standard business**, **ad hoc for crises**.
- Location: Holographic chamber with **remote feed capabilities** for all subsidiaries across galaxies.
- Security: All sessions are recorded in encrypted format; **override modules ensure no external interference**.


---

### **Responsibilities:**
1. Approve major research projects, galaxy-spanning initiatives, and hybridization programs.
2. Supervise financial allocation across subsidiaries and interstellar operations.
3. Evaluate ethical and legal compliance while balancing UL’s operational supremacy.
4. Oversee Protocol Department recommendations and enforce safeguards.
5. Resolve disputes between subsidiaries or departments when conflicts arise.
