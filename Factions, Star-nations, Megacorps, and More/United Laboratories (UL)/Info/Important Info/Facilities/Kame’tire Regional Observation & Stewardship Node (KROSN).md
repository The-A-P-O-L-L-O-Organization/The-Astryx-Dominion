**Primary Function:** Regional coordination for long-range reconnaissance, agro-ecology trials, medical aid, and culturally respectful stewardship with the Kame’tire clan.  
**Base Code:** WFR-KAM / Genesis Vault Tag: KROSN-005  
**Clearance:** Omega (regional coordination) / Mid (research & med) / GC oversight

---

## Site Philosophy & Placement
- **Location:** Positioned at the fringe of Kame’tire territory on low, terraced foothills overlooking mixed savanna/riverine corridors (chosen to maximize line-of-sight for reconnaissance while remaining outside songline corridors and sacred nodes).
- **Design Ethos:** **Quiet Sentinel** — architecture prioritizes observational subtlety, low acoustic profile, and regenerative landscaping that visibly benefits clan hunting and grazing lands.
- **Cultural Considerations:** Pre-deployment consultation with Kame’tire elders and scouts; ceremonial notification and blessing performed. All persistent cultural features and ritual routes preserved.

---

## Core Mission & Objectives
1. **Long-range Recon & Environmental Sensing:** persistent surveillance for ecosystem health, migratory patterns, magnetic anomalies, and RDA legacy artifacts.
2. **Agro-Ecology & Food-Security Trials:** collaborative trials for soil restoration, drought-resilient cultivars, and seed-bank partnerships for Kame’tire subsistence.
3. **Medical Outreach & Field Clinics:** rapid-response medbay for clan aid, epidemic monitoring, and hybrid-protocol rehabilitation (consented).
4. **Cultural Liaison & Apprenticeship:** limited liaison presence; training programs for Kame’tire medics and scouts in restoration tech.
5. **Recon & Rapid Response Staging:** Skyfang and drone staging for search & rescue, reconnaissance, and non-escalatory containment.

---

## Architecture & Layout
### 1. Observation Bastion (OB)
- **Function:** low-profile command & sensor suite; panoramic observation arrays; GAIA-integration node.
- **Design:** terraced, earth-bermed platforms with retractable observation shields to preserve skyline aesthetics.
- **Features:** long-range flux anchors, passive optical hides, acoustic dampers, and Skyfang telemetry consoles.

### 2. Agro- & Seed Cooperative Wing (ASCW)
- **Function:** community-facing demonstration plots, seed vault, micro-irrigation labs, and soil bioreactors.
- **Design:** open terraces with porous foundations to prevent root disruption; integrated rain catchment feeding bioreactors.

### 3. Field Clinic & Med-Eco Lab (FCMEL)
- **Function:** triage, regenerative medicine bay, vaccine cold-chain, and cultural healing room for Kame’tire elders.
- **Design:** modular med units with sterile airflow locks, communal healing alcove co-designed with clan healers.

### 4. Recon Hangar & Drone Gallery (RHDG)
- **Function:** Skyfang staging, UAV swarms, stealth drone repair bays, and rapid sortie launch corridors.
- **Design:** recessed hangars with flux locks and Obscura-compatible signature masking for non-intrusive operation.

### 5. Liaison Pavilion & Training Terrace (LPTT)
- **Function:** meeting space for council interactions, apprenticeship classrooms, and hands-on training for scouts and medics.
- **Design:** open, low-impact pavilions with bilingual interfaces and ritual-friendly layout.

### 6. Seed & Archive Vault (SAV)
- **Function:** dual-key seed bank, cultural record cache (songline metadata reserved per Kame’tire rights).
- **Design:** climate-controlled, subterranean vault with limited access and immutable logging to Genesis Vault.

## Operational Systems & Protocols
### Recon & Sensing
- **Stealth-aware Recon:** sensor pings scheduled to mimic natural diurnal/nocturnal patterns; Skyfang sorties avoid sacred flightlines and songline corridors.
- **Predictive Models:** GAIA-derived migration and drought models guide agro-trial scheduling and community aid.

### Agro & Seed Governance
- **Cooperative Trials:** all crop trials co-designed with Kame’tire food-stewards; success metrics include yield, ecological impact, and cultural acceptability.
- **Seed Sovereignty:** SAV operates under a co-steward license; seed exports off-planet require clan consent + GC audit.

### Medical & Ethical Controls
- **Medical Consent Protocol:** all clinical interventions require informed consent and cultural mediation; emergency life-preservation clause applies when immediate threat to life is present with mandatory post-hoc review.
- **Rehabilitation Programs:** post-protocol follow-up and cultural reintegration sessions available for any hybrid or adaptive treatment performed.

## Power, Comms & Logistics
- **Power:** mixed microgrid — bio-solar terraces, small superconductive peak cells (from UL reserves), and kinetic harvesters for remote sensors.
- **Communications:** flux-locked mesh for local node comms; quantum uplink to PRA & ORCCN; cultural channel for clan communication and ritual scheduling.
- **Logistics:** low-impact Skyfang cargo runs, ground soft-footprint drones on pre-cleared corridors, seasonal supply caching to reduce footprint.

---

## Biosecurity & Environmental Safeguards
- **Quarantine & Decon:** phased decontamination for equipment and personnel leaving agro zones; seed/sample chain-of-custody logged immutably.
- **Non-Transfer & Dual-Key:** biological exports tightly controlled; dual-key access for any genetic or cultural material.
- **Adaptive Monitoring:** live soil microbiome indices and animal migration trackers feed GAIA; operations pause if thresholds breached.

---

## Cultural Integration & Programs
- **Apprenticeship Cascades:** Kame’tire youth apprentices trained in agro-tech, medics trained in regenerative care; exchange scholarships funded by UL.
- **Festival Respect:** base operational tempo reduced or paused during Kame’tire festivals; ceremonial invitations honored.
- **Shared Decision Council:** small governing council (UL director + 2 Kame’tire representatives + Aeonborn) for operational approvals affecting sacred or subsistence resources.

---

## Defensive Posture & Rules
- **Minimal Defensive Presence:** non-lethal deterrents and passive perimeter sensors; Skyfang quick-reaction teams for medical/rescue purposes double as defensive responders.
- **ROE:** non-escalatory posture; lethal force only under immediate life-preservation and with post-hoc Founder/GC notification unless impossibility.
- **Emergency Shielding:** localized Obscura-compatible masking for brief periods if exposure threatens clan safety or base security; used sparingly and logged.

---

## Emergency & Contingency Plans
- **Evacuation Corridors:** pre-mapped Skyfang & ground routes respecting ceremonial paths for safe extraction of civilians and elders.
- **Containment Response:** FCMEL triage + nanomedical lockdown sequences; GAIA rollback triggers for ecological anomalies.
- **Restorative Action Plan:** rapid remedial packages (soil inoculants, seed-replant runs) pre-funded and ready for deployment after incidents.
