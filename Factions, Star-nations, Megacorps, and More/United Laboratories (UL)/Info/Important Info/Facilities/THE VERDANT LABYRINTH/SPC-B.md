Its architecture resembles a kind of cosmic coral reef crossed with a hyper-intelligent organ.

Its structure is built from:

- self-assembling **xeno-chitin load-bearing ribs**
    
- bio-lattice capacitors pulsing with living electrolytes
    
- neural-spore fractals branching like alien forests
    
- hyperplastic “thought tissues” that expand and contract as it processes
    
- isolated microbiomes tuned to specific cognitive functions
    
- a circulatory system that carries information as chemical gradients instead of electrons
    

In some regions the structure looks like bone; in others like a forest canopy; in others like a strange ocean creature frozen mid-motion.

It is beautiful. It is unsettling.  
It is **awake**.
