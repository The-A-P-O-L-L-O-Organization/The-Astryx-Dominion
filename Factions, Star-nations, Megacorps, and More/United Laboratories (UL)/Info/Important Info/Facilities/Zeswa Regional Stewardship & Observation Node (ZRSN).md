## Site Philosophy & Placement
- **Location:** Situated on a high plateau near riverine wetlands, offering clear vantage points while remaining outside ceremonial and hunting paths.
- **Design Ethos:** **Silent Sentinel** — architecture integrates with local flora, using reflective and refractive surfaces to blend into the landscape. Minimal lighting and sound profiles to avoid disturbing wildlife or Zeswa observation practices.
- **Cultural Considerations:** Prior consultation with Zeswa elders ensures base respects spiritual sites and maintains minimal interference with clan activities.

---

## Core Mission & Objectives
1. **Environmental Monitoring:** tracking seasonal changes, river levels, migratory patterns, and local fauna health.
2. **Cultural Liaison:** working closely with Zeswa representatives to coordinate conservation programs and ecological interventions.
3. **Medical & Scientific Support:** field clinic for voluntary treatments, emergency care, and adaptive hybrid research with consent.
4. **Recon & Rapid Response:** Skyfang staging and stealth drones for environmental and defensive operations.
5. **Resource Management:** sustainable research of renewable resources while avoiding sacred or critical zones.

## Architecture & Layout
### 1. Observation Tower

- **Function:** panoramic sensor arrays, environmental monitoring, GAIA-linked coordination node.
- **Design:** terraced, reflective façade; low-visibility to avoid disturbing clan activities.

### 2. Field Lab & Med Bay
- **Function:** triage, genetic analysis, regenerative medicine labs.
- **Design:** modular units with bio-lockdown systems; optional medics’ quarters for extended research.

### 3. Drone & Skyfang Hangar
- **Function:** staging area for stealth drones and Skyfangs; rapid-deployment corridors.
- **Design:** recessed hangars with Obscura-compatible signature masking for non-intrusive operation.

### 4. Liaison Pavilion
- **Function:** meeting and training space for Zeswa collaboration, apprenticeship programs.
- **Design:** low-profile pavilions integrated into natural rock formations; acoustic dampening for private consultations.

### 5. Seed & Ecological Vault
- **Function:** biodiversity vault and archive of ecological data.
- **Design:** subterranean, climate-controlled, dual-key access for sensitive samples and records.
