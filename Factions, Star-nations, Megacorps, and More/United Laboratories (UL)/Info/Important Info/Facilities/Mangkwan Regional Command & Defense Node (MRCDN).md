**Base Code:** HAL-MGW / Genesis Vault Tag: MRCDN-003

**Note:** Placement and operations are designed to avoid cultural interference while providing robust defensive capability; no permanent liaison is embedded per regional strategy.

---

## Site Philosophy & Placement
- **Location:** Emplaced on a geologic outcropping outside direct sight-lines and ritual radii of core Mangkwan settlements. Chosen for minimal ecological disturbance (ash-sparse plateau), good lines-of-sight, and natural cover (charred rock, smoke-veiled ridges).
- **Design Ethos:** **Concealment & Containment** — the base emphasizes low-visibility integration using a reactive stealth shield that renders the base visually and sensor-wise congruent with surrounding ashfields and geothermal haze.
- **Cultural Caution:** Although no liaison is embedded, construction followed initial notification of clan representatives per GC-T.1301 requirements; ceremonial acknowledgement provided to avoid unnecessary provocation.

# Architecture & Layout

## 1. Stealth Envelope & Disguise Field
- **Stealth Shield (“Obscura Array”):** layered force-field system that modulates optical, infrared, radio, and flux signatures to match local ash-haze and thermal gradients.
- **Adaptive Camouflage:** outer surfaces composed of particulate-shedding composites that mimic charred rock and drifting ash when the field is inactive.
- **Operational Modes:**
    - _Passive:_ full disguise (visual+spectral) for peacetime; minimal energy draw.
    - _Active:_ hardened cloak + EM masking during tactical operations; increases draw but maintains disguise under scrutiny.        

## 2. Core Command Bastion (CCB)
- Hardened command center with redundant power, quantum-encrypted comms, GAIA-linked tactical projection, and Omega-level override console.
- Blast-resistant, low-emission architecture with subterranean operations decks to reduce exposure.

## 3. Armory & Defender Barracks
- **Armory (ARMS-1):** secure magazine for non-lethal and lethal systems, exosuit cache (Ecliptic / Obsidian prototypes), Skyfang field-launch munitions, and override-spear docking bay. Dual-key hardware access required.
- **Defender Barracks:** accommodations and rapid-deploy bays for UL Defense Corps: non-lethal crowd-control teams, shielded exo-squads, Skyfang winged units, and armored amphibious frames.

## 4. Tactical Flight & Launch Decks
- Discrete, recessed flight corridors for VTOL and Skyfang launches with flux locks to prevent magnetic interference with floating ash pillars; launch activity suppressed by Obscura Array to preserve stealth.

## 5. Secure Research Annex (SRA)
- Limited-access lab for tactical studies (thermal weaponry containment, ash-environment materials), battlefield medbay for defender casualties, and interrogation‑forensics (strict oversight).

## 6. Logistics & Supply Node

- Armored supply vaults, drone-repair bays, and subterranean cargo tunnels connecting to remote resupply caches (pre-buried for deniability). Automated resupply via low-profile drone trails.

# Defensive Systems & Forces

## Force Composition
- **UL Defensive Squadron (UDS):** small, highly-trained unit (approx. 60 personnel) specializing in rapid strike, base defense, and non-escalatory suppression.
- **Skyfang Contingent:** 4–6 hybrid mounts optimized for ashland flight; trained for low-visibility operations.
- **Ecliptic Exo-Teams:** 2 squads of adaptive exoskeleton personnel for hardpoint defense and breach response.
- **Remote Systems:** turret clusters (non-lethal focused: entanglement, sonic, EMP dampeners) and flux-stabilizing obstacle generators.

## Armament & Non-Lethal Priority
- **Primary emphasis on non-lethal containment:** entanglement web projectors, neural-damp pulse fields, adhesive nets, sonic denial arrays.
- **Lethal options are present but tightly controlled:** kinetic interceptors, high-energy munitions stored under dual-key and Omega-level signed release.
- **Override Spear Presence:** secure docking for authorized Override Spears; activation logs immutable and transmitted to Genesis Vault.

## Rules of Engagement (ROE)
- **Minimize Fatality:** escalate using warning, deterrence, non-lethal immobilization, capture.
- **Stealth Preservation Priority:** operations aim to remain covert unless base under direct attack.
- **Founder/Omega Override:** any lethal escalation or sustained offensive action requires Founder or Omega Council authorization (except immediate life-preservation scenarios under GC-SP.1000, with post-hoc report).

# Operations & Protocols

## Stealth & Non-Interference Protocol
- Base remains under Obscura Passive unless tactical need arises. Patrol routes are restricted to corridors avoiding Mangkwan settlements and ritual lines.
- All sensor pinging minimized and patterned to mimic natural ash/haze cycles.

## Engagement & Capture Workflow

1. **Detection:** sensor flags → commander review → pattern-confirmation via Skyfang recon.
2. **Non-Lethal Containment:** deploy entanglement fields / sonic denial / adhesive nets.
3. **Capture & Secure:** if capture required, move to subterranean holding with medical and ethical oversight. All captures logged and immediately reported to Genesis Vault
4. **Escalation:** lethal contingency sign-off required per ROE.

## Covert Research Restrictions
- No publicized experiments within or near Mangkwan territory that could be construed as provocative. Tactical research confined to SRA and off-site test ranges.

# Legal, Ethical & GC Compliance
- **Authorization:** MRCDN acts under UL custodial mandate; all enforcement documented per GC-R.1200 and GC-ENF.1101.
- **Auditability:** all use-of-force, armory access, and override activations are immutably logged. GC auditors have scheduled access; Ombud channel maintained for whistleblower reporting.

- **Public Incident Response:** predefined press and GC brief templates ready for any exposure event; restorative mission plans pre-budgeted.
