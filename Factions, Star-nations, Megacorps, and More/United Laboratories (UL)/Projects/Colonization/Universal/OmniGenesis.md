**United Laboratories — Project File**  
**Designation:** Project: OmniGenesis  
**Category:** Multiversal Colonization & Habitat Deployment  
**Status:** Planning & Prototype Development

---

### **Overview**

Project: OmniGenesis is designed to establish a UL presence in alternate universes outside the primary universe. This includes deploying modular, self-sustaining habitats, hybrid reconnaissance units, robotic operatives, and integrated AI systems (Nova and SHADOW) to secure resources, map new environments, and begin colonization.

---

### **Objectives**

1. Identify universes suitable for colonization and resource exploitation.
    
2. Deploy autonomous scouting units to perform environmental analysis and risk assessment.
    
3. Establish modular habitats capable of sustaining life and integrating UL technologies.
    
4. Integrate Nova as the central interface for habitat management and SHADOW for digital security and cyber reconnaissance.
    
5. Develop contingency protocols for unknown lifeforms, environmental hazards, and inter-universal anomalies.
    

---

### **Phase I — Scouting & Universe Selection**

- Use hyper-dimensional probes and SHADOW-controlled drones to locate universes with high colonization potential.
    
- Analyze physical constants, elemental composition, energy signatures, and any lifeforms.
    
- Flag universes with extreme risks for future exploration only.
    

---

### **Phase II — Habitat Deployment**

- Modular habitats designed for rapid assembly and autonomous self-sustenance.
    
- Biome-adaptive systems capable of adjusting to atmospheric, gravitational, and ecological conditions.
    
- Nova AI will control internal systems, environmental management, and resident interfaces.
    
- SHADOW AI will maintain network security, prevent interference, and control hybrid/robotic units.
    

---

### **Phase III — Expansion & Integration**

- Gradual deployment of human or hybrid personnel for research and colonization.
    
- Establish UL governance structures, resource harvesting, and research facilities.
    
- Deploy hybrid reconnaissance and defensive units to maintain order and security.
    

---

### **Phase IV — Contingency & Multiversal Governance**

- Develop protocols for universe-specific anomalies and conflicts.
    
- SHADOW AI can remotely neutralize hostile cyber-entities or rogue hybrid/robotic units.
    
- Nova AI ensures operational continuity in habitats and maintains real-time coordination across universes.
    

---

### **Operational Notes**

- All units and habitats equipped with UL-standard communication interfaces.
    
- Multiversal coordinates logged and encrypted within SHADOW.
    
- Ethical oversight protocols adapted to multiversal law are under development, though Founder-level directives may override local ethical constraints in extreme scenarios.
