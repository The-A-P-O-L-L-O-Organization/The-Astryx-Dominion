### Overview

Project: Tulkun transforms consenting subjects into massive, whale-like aquatic hybrids modeled after the tulkun species of Pandora. These hybrids are engineered for high intelligence, advanced social behavior, and bioconnective neural interfaces for cooperative missions. Neural queues allow for interfacing with other compatible hybrids or humans equipped with UL neural implants.

Key traits include:
- Enormous, streamlined body capable of long-distance oceanic travel.
- Reinforced skeletal and muscular structure for underwater endurance and breaching.
- Bioconnective queue formation for synaptic linking with allies or riders.
- Echolocation organs optimized for sonar-based mapping and communication.
- Robust armor plating and adaptive skin coloration for camouflage and protection.
