- Retained human-level reasoning, speech capability (may be altered by vocal-tract shifts), and tool use.

## Control & Containment (Ethics-Forward)

- **Consent & Screening:** Only consenting, fully informed volunteers undergo transformation in canon. Psychological screening required.
    
- **Containment Facilities:** Large tidal test pools and intertidal enclosures mimic natural environments for acclimation.
    
- **Neural Consent Tokens:** If queue/bioconnective interfaces are present, neural consent tokens and per-session limits are mandatory.
    
- **Secretion Management:** Defensive secretions are neutralized by onboard UL antidote patches; handlers must wear adaptive barrier suits until stabilization.
    
- **Reversion / Modulation:** In-setting, reversible modulation protocols exist for short-term adaptations; full reversion is highly resource-intensive and limited.
