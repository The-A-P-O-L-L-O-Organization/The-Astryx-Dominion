Darkvision

Accustomed to low-light conditions, you can see in dim light within 60 feet of you as if it were bright light, and in darkness as if it were dim light.

You have proficiency in the Perception skill.

You have advantage on saving throws against poison and disease, and you have resistance to poison damage.
