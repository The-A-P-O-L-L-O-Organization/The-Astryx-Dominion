United Laboratories — Project File  
Designation: Project: Crociform Ascendant
Category: Hybridization — Heavy Assault / Siege Unit  
Status: Active Testing

---

**Overview**
Project: Crociform Ascendant transforms hosts into alligator-like hybrids, emphasizing brute strength, durability, and aquatic-terrestrial adaptability. These hybrids are intended for frontline combat, environmental control, and direct engagement in hostile territories.

---

**Vector & Delivery**
Vector: Viral-genetic construct integrating crocodilian DNA and musculoskeletal enhancements into the host genome.  
Delivery: Injection, spire-mediated exposure, or aerosolized mutagen.  
Latency: 8–16 hours for initial transformation.

---

**Transformation Stages**
**Stage I (0–8 hours):**
- Rapid skeletal growth begins; host increases in size by 20–30%.
- Skin thickens, developing initial scales and protective armor plates.
- Muscular density increases, priming for raw strength output.

**Stage II (8–16 hours):**
- Host reaches full crocodilian size relative to their age.
- Limbs and tail restructure for quadrupedal movement, swimming, and combat utility.
- Jaw and dentition expand, gaining a full set of strong teeth and crushing capability.

**Stage III (16–24 hours):**
- Full hybrid form realized:
    - Thick, armored hide resistant to small arms fire and blunt trauma.
    - Powerful tail usable for aquatic propulsion, striking enemies, or destabilizing structures.
    - Heightened predatory instincts: ambush tactics, territorial control, and coordinated pack behavior guided by UL override signals.
    - Enhanced sensory systems for detection in murky water or low-light conditions.

Intelligence is retained but may be influenced by instinctual predatory drives.

---

**Tactical Applications**
- **Frontline Assault:** Physical dominance and resilience for breaching enemy defenses.
- **Aquatic Control:** Can patrol rivers, lakes, or swampy regions to disrupt hostile movement.
- **Siege & Subjugation:** Capable of destroying infrastructure and controlling population centers through intimidation and physical power.
- **Pack Operations:** Can operate alongside other Crociform units with instinctive coordination.

---

**Control & Containment**
- Neural implants allow UL remote guidance via Project: Override Spear or Omega Prime clearance.
- Environments must be reinforced for size and weight; host cannot inhabit typical civilian areas safely.
- Feeding requirements are significant; metabolism adapted to high-protein intake for sustained strength

---

**Psychological Notes**
- Predatory instincts are strong; prolonged exposure without training can result in aggressive behaviors.
- Territorial behavior may conflict with UL operational needs if not properly guided.
- Hosts retain cognitive abilities sufficient for strategy but are best deployed in controlled combat zones.
