United Laboratories — Project File  
Designation: Project: Chironomus Shrink  
Category: Hybridization — Stealth & Recon Unit  
Status: Active Testing

---

**Overview**

Project: Chironomus Shrink transforms hosts into cricket-like hybrids, emphasizing extreme miniaturization, agility, and enhanced sensory perception. These hybrids are intended for covert reconnaissance, infiltration, and gathering intelligence in areas inaccessible to normal operatives.

---

**Vector & Delivery**

Vector: Viral-genetic construct integrating orthopteran (cricket) DNA sequences into the host genome.  
Delivery: Injection, aerosolized mutagen, or exposure via UL spires.  
Latency: 4–10 hours for initial transformation.

---

**Transformation Stages**

**Stage I (0–4 hours):**

- Rapid shrinkage begins; host reduces to approximately 1/20th of original size.
    
- Limbs adjust proportionally; musculature adapts for jumping and climbing.
    
- Exoskeletal plates start forming along key body areas for protection.
    

**Stage II (4–8 hours):**

- Wings develop (if host genetics allow), capable of short glides or limited flight.
    
- Antennae grow to improve spatial awareness and environmental sensing.
    
- Eyes adapt for wide-angle vision, motion detection, and low-light navigation.
    

**Stage III (8–24 hours):**

- Full cricket hybrid form realized:
    
    - Small, agile body capable of jumping vast distances relative to size.
        
    - Exoskeleton provides moderate protection against minor physical hazards.
        
    - Auditory and vibrational senses enhanced, allowing detection of nearby activity.
        
    - Neural enhancements maintain intelligence and comprehension despite size reduction.
        

---

**Tactical Applications**

- **Reconnaissance:** Infiltrate spaces inaccessible to larger operatives.
    
- **Espionage:** Observe enemy movements and relay data via neural implants.
    
- **Sabotage:** Capable of accessing machinery, vents, or conduits to disrupt systems subtly.
    
- **Covert Operations:** Miniaturization allows undetected deployment into high-security zones.
    

---

**Control & Containment**

- Neural implants permit remote guidance via Project: Override Spear or Omega Prime clearance.
    
- Requires controlled environments to prevent accidental loss or predation.
    
- Nutrition must be micro-managed due to reduced body size.
    

---

**Psychological Notes**

- Miniaturization can induce disorientation or claustrophobia in some hosts.
    
- Heightened senses may overwhelm inexperienced operators.
    
- Retains full cognitive abilities but must adjust behavior to the constraints of new size.
