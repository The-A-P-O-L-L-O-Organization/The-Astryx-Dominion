Medium humanoid (hybrid), typically chaotic or lawful neutral alignment

Ability Score Increase. Your Strength score increases by 2, and your Dexterity score increases by 1.

Age. Fenrirborn mature like humans but often exhibit signs of accelerated physical peak due to lupine metabolism. Lifespan: 90–120 years.

Alignment. Often chaotic neutral or lawful evil in packs under leadership influence; individual personalities may vary.

Size. Medium. Digitigrade legs give you a slightly taller stance and increased stride.

Speed. Base walking speed 35 ft., climbing speed 20 ft.

Lupine Physiology. You have:

Advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks relying on smell or hearing.

Darkvision 60 ft.

Bite attack: unarmed strike dealing 1d6 piercing damage.


Digitigrade Legs. You have advantage on Acrobatics checks related to jumping or sprinting.

Pack Instinct. When within 30 ft. of at least one allied Fenrirborn, you gain:

+1 to attack rolls and saving throws.

Advantage on initiative checks.

Must obey a pack leader’s orders if designated by DM; disobedience can cause minor stress effects (roleplay/narrative).


Heightened Aggression. Once per short rest, you may enter a Hunt Frenzy for 1 minute:

Advantage on Strength-based attacks and Athletics checks.

Disadvantage on Dexterity saving throws as focus narrows.

Must attack perceived threats each turn if able (DM-guided).


Transformation Instincts. Fenrirborn instinctively hunt in coordinated packs, tracking prey or targets using enhanced senses. You gain proficiency in Survival.

Control Node Vulnerability (Optional Story Element). If subjected to a magical, technological, or narrative “UL Control Beacon,” your loyalty and behavior can be overridden as directed by the controlling entity (UL).

Languages. You can speak, read, and write Common. You can communicate basic commands via growls, whines, or howls with other Fenrirborn.

Equipment Notes. Semi-wolf physiology allows for dual-purpose combat, blending weapons with natural attacks. Armor and equipment may need adjustment for digitigrade leg shape.

Traits Summary:
Fenrirborn are ideal for infiltration, ambushes, and coordinated hunting strikes. Their pack loyalty and enhanced senses make them formidable allies—or terrifying enemies. However, aggression management and obedience to control signals are essential for both roleplay and combat balance.
