## **United Laboratories — Project File**

**Designation:** Project: Vesselus  
**Category:** Hybridization — Void-Resonant Operative Unit  
**Status:** Active Development (classified variant) / Public “Art-Medium” variant (approved for demonstration)

---

### **Overview**

Project: Vesselus is a hybridization initiative that takes inspiration from ancient “Vessels” — bio-constructs of void and soul found in the lore of the Hollow Knight universe. The aim: to create human-compatible hybrid operatives whose physiology incorporates _void-resonant structure_, enhanced soul-field modulation, and shell-reinforced bodies meant for extreme infiltration, psionic resonance, and existential stability in high-void or inter-dimensional zones.

Where prior hybrids were based on animals (insects, moths, etc.), Vesselus draws from a mythic archetype — beings created out of void and soul, designed to contain powerful energies. In UL terms, the hybridization merges human hosts with engineered **Void-Matrix implants**, **soul-resonator composites**, and **shell-reinforcement grafts**.

---

### **Vector & Delivery**

- **Vector:** Viral-genetic construct embedding _resonant void polymer strands_ + _sapient soul-field catalyst_.
    
- **Delivery:** Dual-stage process:
    
    1. Isolation chamber: host is exposed to controlled void-field resonance for 3–6 hours.
        
    2. Injection + cocooning: host is placed into a containment “shell-cocoon” matrix for 24 hours where grafts and implants integrate.
        
- **Latency:** Approx. 12–24 hours for initial stabilization; full operative capability within 48–72 hours.
    

---

### **Transformation Stages**

**Stage I (0–12 h):**

- Skin tone shifts subtly toward pale/ashen with faint void-veining visible under high-magnification scans.
    
- Host experiences “stillness in self” — reduction in spontaneous emotion; mental calm/balance test strongly recommended.
    
- Preliminary soul-field sensors detect echo of “hollow shell” pattern forming in central nervous system.
    

**Stage II (12–36 h):**

- Shell-reinforcement implants integrate along ribcage, skull, and spine — creating bio-armored segments reminiscent of Vessel shell structure.
    
- Soul-resonator composite enters “sync” mode: host begins to project low-level psionic pulsing field (radius ~1 m) usable for sensing void-anomaly or shielding self from psychic bleed.
    
- Host’s eyes may change: pupils fade, iris becomes highly reflective. Vision extends into near-infrared and low-void spectrum.
    

**Stage III (36–72 h):**

- Full operant Vesselus form achieved:
    
    - Bio-shell segments pattern visible (may be hidden beneath clothing).
        
    - Void-field conduit implanted in chest cavity (acts as capacitor/containment).
        
    - Host gains enhanced **void-resonant stability** — able to operate in high-void zones, near black-hole singularities, or psionic shock environments with minimal degradation.
        
    - Psionic active ability: _Shell Echo_ — emits a controlled wave of void-resonant energy causing temporary sensory blackout in a ~3 m radius (duration ~1 s) or disrupts psychic linkage in adversaries.
        

---

### **Tactical Applications**

- **Void-Zone Reconnaissance:** Vesselus hybrids can infiltrate high-radiation, high-void, or inter-dimensional zones that normal biologicals cannot survive.
    
- **Psionic Containment:** Ideal for operations against entities with strong soul-field or void-field presence; their shell implants resist psionic bleed-through.
    
- **Operative Shell Deployment:** Can enter hostile environments, stabilize breaches in psionic/void energy grids, and act as living “containment vessels” for rogue energy.
    
- **Elite Infiltration:** Stealth missions in high-surveillance space, using their passive void-field dampening to evade sensors and psychic detection.
    

---

### **Control & Containment**

- All Vesselus hybrids fitted with standard **Override Implant (Quiet Variant)** for remote command by UL security.
    
- Void-field over-accumulation can lead to **“Shell Fracture”**: host begins to crack their bio-shell segments, risk of void-burst release (lethal). Monitoring required.
    
- Behavioral screening mandatory: host must maintain emotional neutrality during integration; high emotional volatility may cause shell instability.
    

---

### **Psychological & Behavioral Notes**

- Vesselus hosts often describe a **sense of empty stillness** — an echo of their shell architecture; they may feel disconnected from strong emotion for a period.
    
- Some develop _shell-identity syndrome_ — over-identification with their bio-shell, leading to aggressive protection of self and environment (requires counseling).
    
- Many report _void-whispers_ in dream states: faint voices beyond consciousness — possibly residual echo from the void-field conduit.
    

---

### **Public/Art Side Variant**

A lighter version of the hybrid has been released publicly as part of UL’s “Shell-Art Initiative”: genetically modified performers known as **Vesselus-Artists**. These versions lack full tactical augmentations, and instead their shell segments are decorative, and the void-field conduit is reduced to luminous aesthetic effects. They serve in UL’s public outreach and holo-performances.
