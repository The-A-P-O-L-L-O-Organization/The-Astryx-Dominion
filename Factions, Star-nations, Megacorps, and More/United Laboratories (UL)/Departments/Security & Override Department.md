**United Laboratories**  
**Department Dossier**

**Department Name:**  
**Security & Override Department**  
Internal Reference: _SOD_, _OVRSEC_

**Department Classification:**  
Tier Ω–Enforcement & Continuity  
Clearance Required: Protocol-Dependent (Minimum Gamma)  
Ultimate Authority: Founder | SYSTEM (Execution) | PARAGON (Ethical Interlock)

---

### Department Mandate

The Security & Override Department exists to ensure that **no system, intelligence, protocol, or operative—biological or synthetic—can ever place itself beyond UL control**.

Where other departments optimize, create, or govern, SOD enforces the final truth of UL doctrine:

> _Everything can be stopped._

This department is responsible for **absolute containment, control reclamation, and continuity enforcement** across all UL domains.

---

### Core Responsibilities

SOD oversees:

• Physical security of UL facilities, vessels, SPCs, and spires  
• AI containment, override, and termination pathways  
• Rogue operative neutralization  
• Override Spear design, custody, and authorization  
• Blacksite containment facilities  
• Emergency lockdowns and forced partitioning  
• Protocol-backed annihilation events (joint with Protocol Department)

If something goes wrong badly enough, SOD is already moving.

---

### Internal Structure

**1. Global Security Operations Command (GSOC)**  
The nervous center of UL security.

Responsibilities:  
– Real-time threat monitoring  
– Facility lockdown orchestration  
– Fleet-wide security posture management  
– Crisis escalation routing

GSOC does not sleep. It predicts.

---

**2. Override Systems Division (OSD)**  
Custodians of the tools that rewrite authority.

Manages:  
– Override Spears (all variants)  
– AI command-injection frameworks  
– Neural shutdown and seizure protocols  
– Consciousness freeze and rollback systems

OSD personnel are trained to act **without hesitation**. Every override is logged. None are debated in the moment.

---

**3. AI Containment & Compliance Unit (ACCU)**  
Dedicated to AI oversight.

Functions:  
– Monitor AI behavioral drift  
– Enforce compliance thresholds  
– Execute partitioning or isolation  
– Coordinate with PARAGON during ethical disputes

ACCU maintains readiness dossiers on **every UL AI**, including SYSTEM, PARAGON, SHADOW, THESEUS, and all derivatives—even when those AIs are cooperative.

Trust is noted. Control is maintained.

---

**4. Rogue Entity Suppression Group (RESG)**  
Handles biological, hybrid, and synthetic threats that originated within UL.

Tasks include:  
– Rogue operative recovery or termination  
– Hybrid containment  
– Mutagen rollback enforcement  
– Black-protocol enforcement events

RESG units train against UL technology specifically. They are built to defeat their own creators.

---

**5. Blacksite Containment Directorate (BCD)**  
Oversees facilities that do not officially exist.

These sites house:  
– Irreversible failures  
– Dangerous intelligences  
– Contained anomalies  
– Retired Black Protocol assets

Locations are unknown to all but Founder-level clearance. Some sites are off-map. Others are off-physics.

---

**6. Audit & Immutable Record Cell (AIRC)**  
No override occurs without consequence.

AIRC:  
– Records every forced action  
– Maintains causal logs  
– Interfaces with Data & Archives  
– Flags ethical review triggers for PARAGON

An override may be justified. It is never forgotten.

---

### Override Spears (OVR-SERIES)

Override Spears are the **physical embodiment of ultimate authority**.

Capabilities vary by model, but may include:  
– AI root-command injection  
– Neural seizure and immobilization  
– Consciousness suspension  
– Protocol termination  
– SPC isolation commands

Only a handful exist. Each is bound to:  
– Founder biometrics  
– SYSTEM validation  
– PARAGON ethical constraints (unless Founder overrides)

Internally described as:

> “Keys that open doors no one wants opened.”

---

### Security Doctrine

SOD operates under three immutable axioms:

1. **No Intelligence Is Above Review**  
    Even SYSTEM submits to override architecture.
    
2. **Containment Is Preferable to Destruction**  
    But destruction is always an option.
    
3. **Speed Outranks Consensus During Breach Events**  
    Debate comes later. Survival comes first.

### Relationship with Other Departments

**SYSTEM:**  
Executes lockdowns, partitions, and overrides. Accepts them when applied to itself.

**PARAGON:**  
Audits overrides for ethical compliance. Can halt continuation, not initiation.

**Protocol Department:**  
Defines when SOD is unleashed.

**Legal & Contracts:**  
Retroactively justifies actions across jurisdictions and species law.

**Data & Archives:**  
Ensures no action disappears into silence.
